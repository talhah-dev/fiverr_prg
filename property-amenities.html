Property Amenities

Land, recreation, and natural features that define the ranch.

From trails and wildlife to fencing, creek access, trees, springs, and recreational features, the amenities give this Hallettsville ranch broad lifestyle appeal.

Land Highlights

A 50-acre property built for both everyday enjoyment and outdoor use.

The ranch combines open ground, private trails, creek access, and mature trees with the practical benefits of fencing, gated entry, and a park-like front acreage.

Total acreage

50 acres with all four sides fenced and a gated entrance.

Trails throughout

Private trails throughout the property add function, access, and recreation.

Shooting range

A shooting range with berm adds another feature for private outdoor use.

Park-like setting

The upfront seven acres create a welcoming and visually open approach to the ranch.

Trees and growth

The land includes oaks, hickory, cedar, sycamore, dogwoods, and about nine producing pecan trees.

Natural character

Wild mustang grapes, wet and dry creek areas, and spots of natural springs add a strong natural identity to the ranch.

Wildlife presence

Deer, turkey, and rabbit make the property attractive for wildlife viewing and hunting opportunities.

Lifestyle Use

Well suited for a homestead lifestyle or recreational retreat.

The property supports both full-time ranch living and recreational ownership, with practical improvements and open land that can adapt to many different uses.

Whether the goal is privacy, hunting, riding trails, hosting family, or simply enjoying a peaceful Texas setting, the ranch offers a balanced mix of beauty and utility.
